How Zonely calculates the Civic Score for each postcode.

Overall Civic Score (0–1000)

The Civic Score is a weighted composite of 5 bureau categories, each scored out of 100 and then scaled to a 1000-point index. Higher scores indicate stronger civic conditions for property investment.

Confidence Band

Each score includes a confidence band (e.g. ± 32) representing the margin of uncertainty based on data freshness, sample size, and source reliability. A tighter band means higher confidence in the score. Bands are recalculated weekly with each data refresh.
